# Credits

## Music

The alarm sounds used in Napwise are adapted from works by **Javolenus**, available at:
https://ccmixter.org/people/Javolenus

| In-app name | Original title | Artist |
|---|---|---|
| Morning Water | No Plan A | Javolenus |
| Soft Return | Service-charge Blues | Javolenus |
| Still Morning | Weather Or Not | Javolenus |

These works are licensed under the **Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial 4.0 International** license.
https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc/4.0/

Changes made: tracks were renamed and used as gradual-wake alarm sounds within the app.
